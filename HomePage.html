WHO WE ARE

Llamas consist of the vicuna, guanaco, Suri alpaca, and Huacaya alpaca , and the domestic llama. Guanacos and vicunas live in the wild, while alpacas as well as llamas exist only as domesticated animals. Seriously!!! Do you want to know who we are and from where we belong instead of knowing how we will protect the earth from alien invasion using our Llama powers. If your answer is still yes, Let us give you some fun facts about ourself.

We originated from the central plains of North America about 40 million years ago and because of our magical powers we are able to survive the Ice Age and hence still exist on the Earth.

We are very social and intelligent mammals and learn simple tasks after a few repitition. We also have excellent senses for smell, hearing and eyesight used for detection of potential danger.

We live for twenty to thirty years. (As known to humans, but actually after thirty years we move to our home planet (Tractu Galaxy) using "Time Travelling" power)

On Earth we live as mammals help humans to transport materials from one place to another.

Technical Details

For this task, I have decided to built a single web page mainly using HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript. I aimed to keep the design simple yet effective, therefore i have made use of images that communicate with the user to provide aesthetic appeal.I have decided to create all the CSS from the scratch instead of using any responsive framework like bootstrap, etc as i decided to create the whole structure and functionality as per my precise vision. My philosophy while designing my work is to "Keep the design simple but not simpler".
Few Key Points :
1. CSS file is minified for faster page load.
2. Some of the CSS(That I think is important for page ) is placed inside page for optimize CSS delivery.
3. Images are optimized to make sure that the pages are loaded fast.
